For Investors

One entity.
Infinite brands.

The Sid Co. is structured as a single holding company — clean books, one GST registration, one banking relationship, and one point of accountability. Each consumer brand operates independently, reducing brand-level risk while sharing central infrastructure.

  • Single legal entity — simplified due diligence and funding structure
  • Multiple revenue streams across food, exports, and logistics verticals
  • Asset-light brand model — each brand scales without new legal overhead
  • India-first, globally connected — IEC, FSSAI, GST ready

Corporate Structure
Single holding company. All brands are commercial units, not separate legal entities — enabling streamlined governance and one set of audited accounts.
Revenue Diversification
Exports generates B2B international revenue. Chai Corner, DashBite, and SwiftCart generate domestic consumer revenue — distinct demand cycles, resilient as a portfolio.
Growth Path
Franchise expansion, private label products, and a planned artisan marketplace represent three additional revenue layers already in development.
