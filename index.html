Our Standard

Quality Assurance

At The Sid Co., quality is not a department — it is a founding value. Every shipment goes through our 6-step verification process before it leaves India.

1
Supplier Audit & Approval
Before we place a single order, every supplier undergoes a thorough audit — facility inspection, certification verification, past export records, and reference checks. Only approved suppliers enter our network.
2
Sample Approval
A pre-production sample is sent to the buyer for approval before bulk manufacturing or harvesting begins. No bulk order is confirmed without written sample sign-off.
3
In-Process Inspection
For manufactured and processed goods, we conduct mid-production checks — verifying that specifications, packaging standards, and labelling requirements are being followed.
4
Pre-Shipment Inspection (PSI)
100% of our shipments undergo a Pre-Shipment Inspection by a certified third-party inspector. Weight, quantity, quality, packaging, and documentation are all verified before the container is sealed.
5
Lab Testing
For food and agricultural goods, we conduct lab testing for pesticide residues, heavy metals, microbial contamination, and nutritional claims — meeting FDA, EU, and Gulf import standards.
6
Post-Delivery Confirmation
We follow up after every delivery to confirm quality on arrival. Any issue is addressed directly — no deflection, no delay. This is the Siddhanth standard.
